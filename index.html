Settings & data

Green is a small slice of the cycle, so random taps land on red far more often — expect to log a lot before the green arc settles. Tapping at varied times of day is what makes it solvable; always tapping at the same moment tells it nothing. If green and red never separate cleanly, the light is probably actuated (green only when a car trips the loop) and has no fixed schedule.
